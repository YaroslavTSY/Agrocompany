ABOUT US

TBB Trading company was established in Dubai city, UAE as agricultural and wood commodity wholesale trader with high level of customers service from around the globe, by year later our company launched representative office in Ukraine, Odessa. Since foundation our company became one of the leading exporter of sawn timber from Ukraine with a market share of over 25% and we are increasing this figure of market share from year by year.

Today our company owns more than 8000 hectares of land and own storage facilities over 10,000 square meters with 5 hectares of the adjacent territory located in Odessa at a distance of up to 3 km. to the largest seaport in Ukraine and 8 highly equipped sawmills.

Besides our own production, our company purchase different agricultural products directly from Ukrainian farmers all year round, which allows us to fully control the quality and ensure uninterrupted supply of goods in large volumes for our customers.

Today the activities of our company has spread to more than 40 countries and 80 companies around the world with a monthly turnover more than 700 containers of timber and agricultural products, and we are increasing this figure from year to year.

OUR BUSINESS STRUCTURE

Agricultural commodity department:

  1. Farming department - with land bank more than 8000 hectares;
  2. Warehousing department – our private area is more then 5 Ha with 11 000 square meters warehouse including full equipment needed to fulfill any type of loading;
  3. Purchase department – high degree management directly communicated with Ukraine farmers with monthly turnover more then 9000 metric tones of different commodities;
  4. Logistic department – own fleet of trucks and railway wagons with 24 hours services;
  5. Trading department – high qualified traders which are connected with most of TOP food traders and brokers globally. In current year our turnover reached to 400 containers per month and our team improving this result from year to year.

Wood commodity department:

  1. Sawmills department – we are end owners of 8 modern sawmills in the central and western part of Ukraine;
  2. Purchase department – our team personally participating in most of government forestry buying directly from auctions. Monthly we purchase approx 12 000 cbm of pine logs;
  3. Trading department – in 2019 our main market was China but for today our activity reached to more than 10 others countries like: UAE, India, Israel, Korea, Vietnam etc.
  4. Development department – our development department is responsible for both structure, spreading our business globally yearly. Our team participating in more than 10 exhibitions each year and pay high
  5. Our wood commodities yearly turnover is near 4000 containers to more than 10 countries globally;

WHAT WE DO

Official argreement

Before starting any business, be it with a buyer or a broker, our company necessarily signs a contract, which allows to feel more confident and reliable both as a buyer and as a seller. All our business activity happens according GAFTA rules and under control of independent survey company GAFTA member.

Purchase

Due to our well-deserved reputation as a reliable and responsible buyer, we have constant and reliable relationships with Ukrainian manufacturers that ensure uninterrupted supplies of products all year round.

Warehouse delivery

One of the important pricing components are logistics costs and costs associated with warehouse work, due to the fact that our company owns warehouse premises of more than 10,000 square meters, we are more reliable and competitively advantageous than many other companies that often do not have their own warehouse capacities

Special service

All our regular customers are privileged and has special conditions like payment terms, prices, loading conditions and many others services available with us.

Informational support

To provide high level service and be online with all our buyers, our team is available on the calls and e-mail during 24/7. All our buyers will be served and got informational support in max 2 hours from request to response.

Cleaning and packing

To ensure high quality goods for our customers, our team carries out cleaning of goods from any kind of impurities at a high technological level, with possible further packaging of goods in any type of container. Today we carry out packaging of goods in bags 10 kg., 20 kg., 25 kg. and 50 kg., as well as big bags weighing from 500 to 1000 kg. and liner bags.

SURVEY CONTROL

The main responsibility for our company company is quality of our products, so we pays much attention to quality control of the goods when it is taken to the warehouse and during loading to the final buyer. During the purchase and acceptance of goods, our specialists carry out a thorough check and selection of cargo samples, due to this we can obtain exhaustive information on the quality of the cargo even before it reaches our customers.
